## Executive Summary

The 2026-2030 Technician question pool has **409 questions** (down from 411). The FCC VEC made sweeping editorial changes across the entire pool — even "identical" questions got punctuation and wording tweaks. But the changes that matter for study guide content fall into clear categories:

| Category | Count | Study Guide Impact |
|---|---|---|
| Identical (zero changes) | 221 | None |
| Minor wording only | 115 | Questions file update only (auto-generate) |
| Significant rewording | 34 | Questions + explanations need review |
| Topic replacement (same ID, different question) | 27 | Full rewrite of explanation |
| Correct answer changed | 7 | Full rewrite of explanation |
| New questions (no old equivalent) | 5 | New explanations needed |
| Removed questions | 7 | Delete from questions file |
| **TOTAL needing content work** | **73** | **~18% of pool** |

### Key Findings
1. **T1 FCC Rules is the most impacted chapter** — 17 items need work, including 9 topic replacements. The FCC substantially reorganized questions about licensing, beacons, remote control, and control operators.
2. **27 questions kept their ID but are entirely different topics.** This is the sneakiest change — the question IDs match but the content is unrelated to the old question. Every one needs a brand-new explanation.
3. **7 questions changed their correct answer.** Some are just topic swaps (different question on same ID), but T6B11 (gain definition) and T9B01 (connector weatherproofing) are genuine content corrections.
4. **Only 5 truly new question IDs** were added, and 7 were removed. The net pool size dropped by 2.
5. **Group sizes shifted slightly** — 6 groups gained a question, 6 lost one. No groups were added or removed.
6. **T0 Safety is barely touched** — 1 significant reword out of 36 questions. Easy win.
